The workflows

Where the first governed agent goes to work.

Accounts payable

Invoices arrive by email, portal, and PDF. Someone keys them in, matches them, chases approvals, and schedules payment. It is slow, error-prone, and the exact place invoice fraud lives.

What the agent does
Reads incoming invoices wherever they arrive, extracts and codes them, matches them against orders and receipts, flags anything that does not line up, and schedules payment for what does.
What Nien enforces
Spend limits per supplier and per payment. Two approvals above your threshold. No payment to an unverified bank account. Duplicate detection before scheduling, not after.
What the owner sees
A queue of what was paid, what is waiting on approval, and what was blocked and why, with the full trail behind every line.

Bookkeeping and reconciliation

The books are always a few weeks behind reality, and reconciliation eats the days before every close.

What the agent does
Works as a shadow bookkeeper. It builds a parallel set of books from your bank feeds, invoices, and statements, and reconciles them daily. It writes nothing to your ERP until its parallel books have matched yours, entry for entry, over a period you choose.
What Nien enforces
Read-only access until the agent has earned write access on its record. Even then, entries above a materiality threshold wait for review.
What the owner sees
A daily match report between the shadow books and the real ones, and a clear line on when, and whether, to let the agent write.

Collections and receivables

Overdue invoices sit because chasing them is nobody's favorite job, and cash suffers for it.

What the agent does
Tracks every open invoice, sends reminders on your schedule and in your tone, escalates the accounts that stop responding, and prepares the picture your team needs before a call.
What Nien enforces
Approved templates only. No discounts, waivers, or payment-plan terms beyond what you have authorized. Named accounts can be marked hands-off entirely.
What the owner sees
Days-sales-outstanding trending, every message the agent sent, and every case it escalated to a person.

Treasury and spend oversight

Cash lives across banks and entities, and by the time anyone assembles the full position, it has changed.

What the agent does
Maintains a live cash position across accounts, forecasts short-term needs from payables and receivables, and proposes transfers to keep balances where policy says they should be.
What Nien enforces
Proposals only, until you decide otherwise. Any transfer it is ever allowed to make alone is capped, restricted to accounts you own, and subject to the same approval rules as a person.
What the owner sees
The position, the forecast, and every proposal with the reasoning attached.
