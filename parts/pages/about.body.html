About

Agents will do real work inside companies. On the company's terms or not at all.

We started Nien on three convictions.

What we believe

Three convictions, one product.

Conviction 1

Autonomous agents are going to carry real operational work: paying invoices, keeping books, managing cash. Not demos. The actual work.

Conviction 2

That only happens if the company stays in control. Not "control" as a dashboard bolted on after an incident, but rules the company owns, checked the same way every time, on every action, from the first day. Trust in an agent should be earned on a record anyone can inspect, the way it is earned by a person.

Conviction 3

Control should not be a luxury good. The biggest vendors sell governance inside their own walls at prices set for the Fortune 500. Most of the world's businesses run on a mix of systems those vendors will never see, and they deserve the same safety without the ransom.

Nien is that control plane: between your data and your agents, on your side of the table.
